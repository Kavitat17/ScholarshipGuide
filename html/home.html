IMPORTANCE OF SCHOLARSHIPS

Scholarship is a critical tool to facilitate education for eligible students ,especially those who are socially and economically challenged in the state and bring them on to the mainstream development track. However, for availing such scholarships it depends largely on the level of awareness among the students about the scholarship schemes. Though a considerable number of scholarship schemes are available in Maharashtra, level of awareness regarding scholarship among students is not known. The present study was conducted to assess the awareness of Technical ,Medical Universities and Higher Class students on different Scholarship Schemes.
